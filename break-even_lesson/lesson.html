☕ Break-Even Explained — Console.log Café

1. The Core Question

Every café owner must answer one question:

How many items do I need to sell before I stop losing money?

That point is called Break-Even.

2. Your Café Costs

Fixed Costs (monthly):

  • Rent: $300
  • Equipment: $200
  • Utilities: $100

Total Fixed Costs = $600

Variable Cost per coffee = $1.00

Selling Price = $4.00

3. Contribution Margin

Contribution = Price - Variable Cost

4.00 - 1.00 = $3.00 per coffee

Each coffee contributes $3 toward paying your fixed costs.

4. Break-Even Formula

Break-even = Fixed Costs ÷ Contribution

600 ÷ 3 = 200 coffees per month

That means:
👉 About 7 coffees per day to break even.

5. Try It Yourself

6. Real Business Insight

If you sell:

  • Less than break-even → You lose money
  • Equal → You break even
  • More → You make profit
Break-even is not theory — it's your survival line.
